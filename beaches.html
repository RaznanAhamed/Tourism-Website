Explore the beautiful coast

The tropical beaches of Sri Lanka are by far the best beaches in South Asia. Sri Lanka posses a 1340km(833 miles) coastline. The beaches in Sri Lanka varies topographically attracting different types of people like surfers, sun seeker and fun lovers throughout the year. Sri Lankan beaches are also homeland for many marine endangered turtles, whales and dolphins. Hence, giving tourists the opportunity to witness them at sea.

Unawatuna

Unawatuna is still one of the most sought beaches for swimming and jet sking in Sri Lanka. Its azure calm water and the offshore reef is the best for relaxation. Although Unawatuna gets busy during peak season it never feels crowded. Unawatuna got weekend beach parties too for you fun lovers.

Dalawella

Just east of Unawatuna, this beach has got amazing light yellowish sand on which huge rocks preside. Since Dalawella's lagoon is protected by a reef you can swim or paddle throughout the year. This beach is an amazing spot for families and the people who are curious to witness turtles since they make random appearances.

Mirissa

Most of those who visit the infamous Mirissa beach are here for the whales and for half day cruises. Yet it offers much more activities such as snorkeling , taking a yoga class or enjoy the wonderful scenery that'll bring coolness to your eyes.
