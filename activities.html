Activities to engage in Sri Lanka

Whether you’re looking for an adventure atop rugged mountain peaks or a relaxing getaway on the soft sand of the country's best beaches, there’s nothing like a holiday in Sri Lanka.

Surfing

The most popular surfing destination in Sri Lanka is the south east beach settlement of Arugambay. Arugambay is listed as one of the top 10 surfing spots in the world. Arugambay is surfer friendly and has plenty of affordable accommodations.

Surf Packages

Adult USD 32

Child USD 16

Whale Watching

Enjoy a luxury whale watching experience on a shared catamaran in the east coast of Sri Lanka from May to October every year. This excursion is ideal for families and group of friends or colleagues.

DEPARTURE: Dutch Bay, Trincomalee

RETURN: Dutch Bay, Trincomalee

Duration: 7 hours

The Wildlife Department of Sri Lanka will be charging a fee for all the whale watching cruises.

  • Foreign Adult: USD 18
  • Foreign Child: USD 9
  • Local Adult: LKR 200
  • Local Child: LKR 125

Wildlife Safari

The Yala National Park is often described as the finest safari destinations of Sri Lanka having one of the highest densities of wildlife per square kilometer. This park is home to vast variety of animals, elephants and leopards are one of them.

Package duration 4 days - 3 nights

Cost USD 478 per person
